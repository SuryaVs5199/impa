Our Doctor's Wings Activities

The Doctors Wing aims to bring together medical professionals from various specialties to collaborate and support the community's healthcare needs. 
The IMPA Doctors Wing plays a crucial role in promoting the well-being of community members and ensuring that they have access to quality healthcare services.

Community Health Awareness

Organizing health camps, seminars, and workshops to raise awareness about various health issues and promote preventive healthcare practices within the community.

Medical Services

Providing medical consultation and services to community members in need, especially those who may face financial constraints.

Medical Education

Encouraging and supporting aspiring medical students from the community by providing guidance and scholarships.

Social Initiatives

Undertaking social initiatives related to healthcare, such as blood donation drives, health check-up camps, and support for medical emergencies.

Networking and Collaboration

Creating a network of medical professionals within the community to collaborate and share knowledge and expertise.

Community Health Directory

Establishing a directory of medical professionals from the community to help members find appropriate medical services easily.

Research and Development

Encouraging medical research and innovations that can benefit the community and society at large.
