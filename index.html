Our Wings

The Impa organization has a women's wing, a youth wing, a doctors wing, a lawyers wing and a B2B wing are operating efficiently:

Women's Wing

The women's team has been formed and is working to integrate all the women in the society. From time to time special meetings are held to bring together the women of the community who are pioneers in our society.

Know More

Youth wing

The Impa Youth wing has been formed for the unity and development of the youth of our community and Mr. G.V. Selvam, Vice President, VIT University, Vellore is the leader of this youth wing.

Know More

Doctor's Wing

Impa Clinic is working with a team of voc doctors to bring together all the doctors in our community for further emergency treatment in order to meet the medical need of the community people.

Know More

Advocates Wing

The Impa Youth wing has been formed for the unity and development of the youth of our community and Mr. G.V. Selvam, Vice President, VIT University, Vellore is the leader of this youth wing.

Know More

B2B Business To Business

Impa State Treasurer Mr. Appu R. Chandrasekhar is the Chief Coordinator for the B2B organization which includes business leaders in our community to ensure the growth of the community. Impa B2B operates in 13 districts namely Chennai, Kanchipuram, Vellore, Dharmapuri, Salem, Karur, Erode, Tiruppur, Coimbatore, Dindigul, Tirunelveli, Madurai and Bangalore.

IMPEC (International Mudaliarpillaimar Education Consortium)

IMPEC was formed under the leadership of Mr. Baladantayuthapani, President of Senkunthar Educational Institutions, to coordinate our community educators and provide them with the assistance and advice they need.
