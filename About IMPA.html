About Us

We People focus on promoting unity, entrepreneurship, education, and social welfare among our community members. 
Our IMPA is a prominent and diverse community in Tamil Nadu with a deep-rooted cultural heritage.

District Offices

Offices have been set up in 18 districts in Tamil Nadu where five computers and a sewing machine and 50 seats have been provided to train a women and youth in the community.

IMPA Executive Committee

IMPA Executive Committee members attend about 675 meetings to meet community people in all districts of Tamil Nadu. They have traveled about 4,05,500kilometers to attend the meeting and have met about 1,68,750 people in person
